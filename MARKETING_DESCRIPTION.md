## Key Marketing Messages

### Problem/Solution Framework

**Problem 1:** Traditional flashcards promote shallow memorization
**Solution:** AI guidance helps you understand the "why" behind each answer

**Problem 2:** Too many cards to review, can't prioritize effectively  
**Solution:** Smart "Done" system moves mastered material out of the way

**Problem 3:** Need to study offline but apps require constant internet
**Solution:** Export to Markdown and study anywhere

**Problem 4:** Expensive competitor apps with features you don't need
**Solution:** Clean, focused interface with fair pricing

- Built-in AI tutor (no complex plugins)
- Cloud sync included (no setup required)
- Mobile-friendly web app (works everywhere)

### Competitive Advantages vs. Quizlet
- Medical-focused, not generic
- AI that teaches, not just tests
- Privacy-focused (your data stays yours)
- No ads or distractions
